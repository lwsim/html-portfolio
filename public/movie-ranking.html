The Best Movies According to Sim

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Inception

It explores the depths of the human subconscious through a unique concept of dream within a dream.

My Friend Dahmer

It explores the troubled adolescence of the infamous serial killer before he committed his notorious crimes.

Hidden Figures

It tells the true story of three African American women who were very important in helping NASA send an astronaut into space, despite facing unfair treatment because of their race and gender.
